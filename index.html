About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Liaisan Bradshaw, a Computer Science graduate from Wilmington University with a passion for software engineering. I have a broad range of technical skills, including proficiency in PHP, Java, .NET, C#, Python, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. I am a dedicated problem solver who thrives on challenges and is always looking for new ways to improve my skills.

Outside of my professional life, I am an avid traveler who loves to explore new cultures and experiences. I am also a devoted fan of Joy Division, and music plays a big role in my life. I find inspiration in the energy of live music, and I often use it as a way to unwind after a long day of coding.

As you browse my portfolio, you'll see examples of my work and how I apply my technical skills to real-world projects. You'll also get a sense of my personality and interests, which I believe are an essential part of who I am as a software engineer.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you and bring your projects to life!
